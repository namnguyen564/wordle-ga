How To Play:

  • NEW FIVE LETTER WORD ON LOAD/RETRY
  • THE COLOR OF THE TILES WILL CHANGE TO SHOW HOW CLOSE YOUR GUESS WAS TO THE WORD
  • GREEN BOXES MEAN THE LETTER IS IN WORD AND CORRECT SPOT
  • YELLOW BOXES MEAN THE LETTER IS IN THE WORD BUT INCORRECT SPOT
  • GREY BOXES MEAN THE LETTER IS NOT IN THE WORD
